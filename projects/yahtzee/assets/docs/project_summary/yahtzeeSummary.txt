- This is a 1 player game in which you roll dice up to 3 times per turn and attempt to get the best possible score based on the combination of dice you get.<br><br>
- You start a new game by clicking "New Game". Roll until the score button changes. Place your score in the scoresheet, click score, and it will roll the dice for your next turn.
You do not need to calucalte anything. Possible scores for each turn as well total scores are caclulated for you.<br><br>
